How it Works

1. Nominate someone or an organization

Only available to NJ residents/organizations who have done good deed(s).
This can be an isolated situation or something ongoing - whatever you consider “good deeds”

Awards will range from $500 - $1000

We're accepting submissions from September 15, 2023 to November 15, 2023

2. Start the form

Follow the steps and fill out the form below to tell us the GOOD you have done! If you have any difficulty with the submission process or get an error notification, you are welcome to submit your nomination manually in an email to chasinggoodnj@gmail.com

The submission period for 2023 has closed. Please check out the website in late December to see our winners!

3. Entries will be judged

Entries will be judged by the Chasing Good Voting Committee

4. Sit back and wait

Sit back and know that it pays to do good!

If you have any problems submitting a nomination, you can also email us the info at chasinggoodnj@gmail.com.

5. We contact potential winners

We will contact potential award winners to let them know or we may email/call you for further information.

6. Winners will be notified

Award winners of the Chasing Good Award(s) will be notified and mailed a check.
