How it Works

1. Nominate someone, yourself, or an organization

Only available to NJ residents/organizations who have done good deed(s).
This can be an isolated situation or something ongoing - whatever you consider “good deeds”

Awards will range from $500 - $1000

Please note that the submission period has closed for 2022. Please come back in the fall and see who won and also visit us for another chance in 2023!

2. Start the form

Follow the link below and fill out the form to tell us the GOOD you have done!

3. Entries will be judged

Entries will be judged by the Chasing Good Voting Committee

4. Sit back and wait

Sit back and know that it pays to do good!

Please email us at chasinggoodnj@gmail.com if your submission doesn't go through so we can fix any problems.

5. We contact potential winners

We will contact potential award winners to let them know or we may email/call you for further information.

6. Winners will be notified

Award winners of the Chasing Good Award(s) will be notified and mailed a check.
