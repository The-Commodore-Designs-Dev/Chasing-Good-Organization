Greetings

It all started when Barbara and Nick married and raised their three daughters in Westfield, NJ. As the girls grew older they witnessed their parents perform countless good deeds and selfless acts of kindness. The girls remember their mother, a teacher, offering to help out a struggling family in the district. Their girls watched as Barbara and Nick welcomed people into their home, played a huge role in the church and always thought of others before themselves. Their girls; Michele, Christine, Antonia and later Johannah learned the impact and importance of volunteerism, which would ignite a passion in all of the girls. The idea of doing good and helping others was eventually passed on to the next generation of 9 grandchildren ranging from 25-13 years old.
