How it Works

1. Nominate someone, yourself, or an organization

Only available to (NJ residents/organizations only please) who has done good deed(s). This can be an isolated situation or something ongoing- whatever you consider “good deeds”

Submit the form by 12/31/2021

2. Start the form

Follow the steps and fill out the form below to tell us the GOOD you have done!

3. Sit back and wait

Sit back and know that it pays to do good!

4. We contact potential winners

We will contact potential winners to let them know or we may email/call you for further information.

5. Winners will be notified

Winners of the Chasing Good Award(s) will be notified and mailed a check (TBD if we will be able to have an awards ceremony in 2022).
