Here's How it Works

  1. Nominate someone, yourself, or an organization (NJ residents/organizations only please) who has done good deed(s). This can be an isolated situation or something ongoing- whatever you consider “good deeds”
  2. Fill out the form below.
  3. Submit the form by 12/31/21.
  4. We will contact potential winners to let them know or we may email/call you for further information.
  5. Winners of the Chasing Good award(s) will be notified and mailed a check (TBD if we will be able to have an awards ceremony in 2022).
  6. Sit back and know that it pays to good!
